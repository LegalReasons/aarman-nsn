The Nation

About Aarman

The Grand Imperial Republic of Aarman stands as a sovereign nation built upon the dual pillars of imperial tradition and republican representation. Founded in the spirit of unity, Aarman recognizes the sacred bond between ruler and subject — a covenant expressed in the gold, indigo, and white of its national colors.

The Emperor serves as the supreme symbol of the state and its people, while the Republic ensures that governance flows from the will of Aarman's citizens. Together, they form a singular, enduring civilisation.
