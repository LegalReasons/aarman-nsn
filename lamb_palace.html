Emperor Shane I is the reigning sovereign of the Grand Imperial Republic of Aarman, head of the ancient and storied House of Lamb, whose lineage has shaped the Empire across generations. Ascending to the Throne in Shandria, Emperor Shane I has governed in the tradition of his forebears — firm in authority, faithful to the covenant between ruler and people enshrined in the Principal Articles.

His Imperial Majesty presides over the executive administration of the Empire from Lamb Palace, directing the Primarium, commanding the uniformed services, and conducting the foreign affairs of Aarman with the full dignity of the imperial office.

Seat of the Throne

Lamb Palace

Lamb Palace stands at the heart of Shandria, the imperial capital of Aarman, as the official residence and principal executive office of the Emperor. Its soaring colonnades and grand ceremonial halls — hewn from the distinctive red and pink granite quarried from the mountains of the surrounding region — have made it one of the most recognizable structures in the Empire.

Constructed under a past Emperor of the House of Lamb, the Palace bears the dynasty's name in both honor and legacy. Its name carries a second significance, however: the lamb, Aarman's national animal, is a symbol of the people themselves — and so the Palace, home to the Shepherd of Aarman's Lands and Peoples, speaks to the covenant at the heart of the Empire.

Beyond its ceremonial function, Lamb Palace houses the offices of the Primarium, the Emperor's principal advisors, and serves as the venue for state receptions, treaty signings, and addresses to the nation. The Palace grounds, open to citizens on designated days, are a cherished institution of public life in Shandria.
